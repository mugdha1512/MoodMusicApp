About Mood Music

Mood Music is an AI-powered web app that uses facial recognition (via DeepFace) to detect your current emotion from a webcam snapshot and curates personalized Spotify-like playlists to uplift or match your vibe. Built with Flask, it's your daily companion for emotional wellness through music.
